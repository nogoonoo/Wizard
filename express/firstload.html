Welcome to your new Greenscreen Display!

Welcome to the Greenscreen Display Settings page. From here, you can customize your Greenscreen in several ways:


You can integrate your Google, Office 365, or iCloud calendar (or other 'iCal' format) to Greenscreen. With Greenscreen's touch capabilities, you can view appointment details or view details for the entire day.
Powered by weather.gov, you can see conditions for your location and customize your forecast. You can also view a live weather map.
Greenscreen allow you to add images from different sources: stock background images, iCloud shared albums, Bing wallpapers, or images directly uploaded via your personal device to Greenscreen.
Greenscreen's news ticker features lets you view both pre-configured news feeds from AP, the New York Times, and other sources. Additionally, you can add your own RSS feeds to Greenscreen. Clicking on a news article lets you read it in a modal window.
Greenscreen comes with a "Date Countdown" and a "Word of the Day" from the Merriam Webster's dictionary. Want a new widget? Let us know and maybe it will show up in a future update.
You can add a custom message in the sidebar or center of your Greenscreen.
Your Greenscreen display's features are divided into different sections of the screen:




All these features are configured via the Greenscreen Display Settings by clicking on the bottom-right of your screen
You can access settings and additional features via the button in the bottom-right of Greenscreen:




From the slide-out menu, you can change the background image, set a timer, reboot Greenscreen, or access the configuration wizard.
Greenscreen settings can be accessed via laptop, smart phone, or tablet by scanning the QR Code in the slide-out menu. Note that the device must be on the same network as Greenscreen.
You can access Greenscreens configuration wizard at any time by scanning the QR code found in 2 places:


1 - The top-right corner of any Configuration page
2 - In the slide-out menu of your Greenscreen display




Certain settings (like uploading background images) can only be accessed on personal devices. Also, copy/paste and entering text may be easier on a personal device than by inputting text using the on-screen keyboard.
